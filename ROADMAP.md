### Unified Media Encoding

A long-term objective is the ability to encode the same encrypted payload throughout an entire media asset.

For example, a video could contain the encrypted payload within:

- Visual frame data
- Audio tracks
- Metadata structures
- Frequency-domain coefficients

This would create significant redundancy and potentially allow recovery even if portions of the media are modified, compressed, transcoded, or partially damaged.

### Bitcoin-Backed Media

Future research may explore embedding encrypted Bitcoin-related information into:

- Images
- Audio
- Video
- Multi-media collections

The ultimate vision is to investigate whether digital media can become a carrier of cryptographic value, ownership information, and long-term preservation incentives.

### Media Rights Management

Future multisignature systems may allow:

- One key embedded in the media
- One key held by the issuer
- Time-locked redemption schedules
- Long-term holding incentives
- Collector reward mechanisms

Such systems could create new forms of digital ownership and value preservation while discouraging unauthorized redistribution.
